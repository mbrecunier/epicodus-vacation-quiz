Vacation Quiz

You should...

Fly over to Iceland!

It sounds like you might enjoy the breathtaking nature of Iceland. You can check out Aurora Borealis, geothermal spas, endless hiking, and cultural museums.

  • The hot spot: The capital, Reykjavik, is full of great food, shopping, music, nightlife, and conveniently located hotels
  • Don't miss: Blue Lagoon, a geothermal spa about a half hour from Reykjavik
  • Average flight cost from NY: $800

Check out the beaches in Thailand!

Beaches, food, beaches, beaches, water, food, what else do you need?

  • The (not-too) hot spot: Mai Khao, Phuket is near the airport and less rowdy than other beaches in the area
  • Don't miss: The water park at Centara
  • Average flight cost from NY: $500

Hit the slopes in Whistler!

You already know where you want to go. Forget the spas and relaxation. You want to put on your three warmest jackets and spend your days zooming down the mountain until you can't feel your legs.

  • The hot spot: For the brave and experience skiier, Surf's Up boasts the best snow on the mountain
  • Don't miss: Nita Lake Lodge- we know you are playing in the snow all day, but don't forget about the luxury offerings
  • Average flight cost from NY: $180

Just stay home and avoid disappointment...

Are your expectations too high? It sounds like you want too many things different things and we recommend just forgetting the vacation. It is better to be disappointed at home than to be disappointed and thousands of dollars poorer in another country.

  • The hot spot: The left side of the couch under the wool blanket you got for Christmas in 2001
  • Don't miss: Law and Order SVU marathons
